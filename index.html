Do I have to be a UNT student to leave a review?

Yes! In order to ensure that the reviews submitted are accurate to the experiences of the student body, we only allow those with a valid UNT student email to leave reviews.

Will my professors know who wrote it?

Nope! Although you must have an account to leave a review, all reviews are posted anonymously with only you being able to see your past reviews.

Can I change a review after posting?

No. If you change your mind after submitting a review you have the option to delete. If you would like, you can leave another review afterwards.

Could I use my EUID or UNT ID instead of my student email?

No. At the moment the only accepted form of student verification is your UNT issued student email. 'firstnamelastname@my.unt.edu'

Why would I use this when the SPOT evaluations exist?

Good question! SPOT evaluations serve one purpose: to give feedback to professors and UNT faculty. Our goal is to give some of that back to the students so that you can make informed decisions and go into semesters prepared.
